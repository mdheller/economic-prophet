# Economic Profit White Paper

This repository hosts the open, auditable **Economic Profit** framework and its reference implementation.

## Current manuscript status

The working manuscript developed in this project covers:
- Economic Profit as a coherent value-based management framework
- banking entity and regulatory boundary conditions
- financial valuation theory, EP vs FCF, time horizon, and terminal value
- CAPM, beta, measures of risk, and their empirical shortcomings
- funds transfer pricing, hurdle rates, and pricing logic
- regulatory capital, risk capital, and economic capital
- expected loss, unexpected loss, PD / LGD / EAD, and credit model families
- relationship, account, portfolio, product, and enterprise profitability
- central banking, monetary regime, distribution choice, circuit breakers, contagion, and blast radius
- duration, convexity, Taylor expansion, PDE framing, and Ross-style recovery integration

The canonical draft is being normalized into an open specification and executable reference code in this repository.
